Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Co-Parenting Calendar ("the App") is designed to help families organise shared parenting schedules and activities. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Your email address (used for authentication via Google Sign-In).
  • Calendar events and schedules you create.
  • Family and child information that you enter.
  • Other content you choose to create within the App.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your account.
  • Synchronise your family's shared calendar.
  • Provide the core functionality of the App.

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with the cloud services required to operate the App, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore provided by Google.

Data Security

Data is transmitted using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Reasonable measures are taken to protect your information.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account from within the App by opening Settings → Delete Account. Deleting your account removes your authentication account and associated personal data.

Additional information is available on our Account Deletion page.

Your Rights

You may delete your account at any time using the in-app account deletion feature.
