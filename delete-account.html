Delete Your Account

Users can permanently delete their Co-Parenting Calendar account directly from within the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open Co-Parenting Calendar.
  2. Sign in.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion.

Deleting your account permanently removes your authentication account and associated personal data from our systems. If you are the final member of a family, the associated shared family data will also be deleted.

If you are unable to access the app, contact the developer for assistance.
